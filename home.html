Our targets.

Minimize and address the impacts

of ocean acidification, including

through enhanced scientific

cooperation at all levels.

By 2020, effectively regulate

harvesting and end overfishing,

illegal, unreported and

unregulated fishing

By 2020, sustainably manage and

protect marine and coastal

ecosystems to avoid significant

adverse impacts

By 2025, prevent and significantly

reduce marine pollution of all kinds, in

particular from land-based activities

By 2020, conserve at least 10 per cent of

coastal and marine areas, consistent with

national and international law and based on

the best available scientific information
